# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone.

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable feedback, and focus disagreement on ideas and evidence. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualised conduct, or sustained disruption are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behaviour harms the community or project. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository maintainers rather than in a public issue.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
